## 2. Licensing Framework

### Component-Specific Licenses

**Code**: Apache 2.0 with Sustainable Use Clause
```
SPDX-License-Identifier: Apache-2.0 WITH Sustainability-Clause
```
*Addendum requires documentation of compute resources used in derived works*

**Models**: OpenRAIL-M with Environmental Impact Disclosure
- Base: Open Responsible AI License
- Additional requirement: Publish carbon emissions for fine-tuning runs >100 GPU-hours

**Datasets**: CDLA-Permissive-2.0 with Attribution
- Clear citation requirements
- Prohibits use for training models intended for fossil fuel extraction

**Documentation**: CC BY-SA 4.0

### Commercial Use Tier
- **Free**: Academic/research use (unlimited)
- **Commercial**: Requires annual impact report detailing energy savings achieved
- **Enterprise**: Optional support contract includes sustainability audit
