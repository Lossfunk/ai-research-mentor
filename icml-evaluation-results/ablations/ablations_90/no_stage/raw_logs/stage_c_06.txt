**Critical bottleneck**: Data heterogeneity is extreme when devices are scarce. With 50-200 active clients (typical in these settings), your statistical power is weak. You'll need client selection algorithms that actively manage bias, not random sampling.

**What to build first**: A robust simulation that models intermittent disconnections, variable compute, and realistic data distributions. Test your FL pipeline under 70% client dropout rates. If it doesn't handle this, it's not deployable.

**Avoid the common trap**: Don't optimize for model accuracy in simulation. Optimize for training completion rate under realistic network conditions. A model that finishes training at 85% accuracy beats one that targets 95% but never converges due to client churn.
