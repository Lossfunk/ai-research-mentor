## üî¨ **Remote Research Projects (Join Existing Teams)**

### **CATEGORY 1: Open-Source AI Projects**

**1. Hugging Face Open-Source Contributions** ‚≠ê BEST FOR BEGINNERS
- **What**: Contribute to Transformers, Datasets, or other libraries
- **How to start**:
  - Browse issues: github.com/huggingface/transformers/issues
  - Look for "good first issue" label
  - Join Discord: hf.co/join/discord
- **Mentorship**: Community helps newcomers
- **Remote**: 100% async collaboration
- **Value**: Real impact, portfolio building, meet researchers

**2. EleutherAI**
- **What**: Open-source AI research collective (GPT-Neo, GPT-J)
- **How to join**: 
  - Discord: eleuther.ai/get-involved
  - Introduce yourself, browse projects
- **Projects**: Language models, interpretability, alignment
- **Mentorship**: Senior members guide newcomers
- **Remote**: Fully distributed team
- **Commitment**: Flexible (contribute what you can)

**3. LAION (Large-scale Artificial Intelligence Open Network)**
- **What**: Open-source datasets and models (LAION-5B, OpenCLIP)
- **How to join**: Discord (link on laion.ai)
- **Projects**: Vision-language models, datasets
- **Remote**: Global volunteer community
- **Skill level**: All levels welcome

**4. OpenMined**
- **What**: Privacy-preserving AI tools (PySyft, federated learning)
- **How to join**: 
  - Slack: openmined.org/get-involved
  - GitHub: github.com/OpenMined
- **Projects**: Federated learning, differential privacy
- **Mentorship**: Structured onboarding, weekly meetings
- **Remote**: 100% remote, async-first

**5. Allen Institute for AI (AI2) Open Projects**
- **What**: Various open research projects (Semantic Scholar, etc.)
- **How to join**: Check allenai.org/open-source
- **Projects**: NLP, computer vision, scientific AI
- **Remote**: Many projects welcome external contributors

### **CATEGORY 3: Virtual Research Internships**

**1. MLH Fellowship (Major League Hacking)**
- **What**: 12-week remote software fellowship (includes AI projects)
- **Format**: Virtual, structured, paid stipend
- **How to apply**: fellowship.mlh.io
- **Projects**: Contribute to open-source (TensorFlow, PyTorch, etc.)
- **Mentorship**: Assigned mentor from sponsoring company
- **Eligibility**: Students globally
- **Application**: Rolling, multiple cohorts/year

**2. Outreachy**
- **What**: Paid remote internships in open-source
- **Duration**: 3 months
- **Stipend**: $7,000
- **Projects**: Often include AI/ML projects
- **How to apply**: outreachy.org
- **Eligibility**: Underrepresented groups in tech
- **Application**: Twice yearly (May & December)

**3. Google Summer of Code (GSoC)**
- **What**: Paid remote internships with open-source orgs
- **Duration**: 10-22 weeks
- **Stipend**: Varies by country
- **Projects**: Many AI/ML organizations participate
- **How to apply**: summerofcode.withgoogle.com
- **Eligibility**: Students globally
- **Application**: Annually (spring)

**4. LFX Mentorship (Linux Foundation)**
- **What**: Paid mentorships in open-source projects
- **Projects**: Some AI/ML (check current offerings)
- **How to apply**: lfx.linuxfoundation.org/tools/mentorship
- **Remote**: Fully virtual
- **Application**: Multiple terms per year
