### **Project Ideas (Ethically Focused):**

**Beginner:**
1. **Bias Detection in Image Classifiers**
   - Use pre-trained model (ImageNet)
   - Test on diverse faces/objects
   - Document biases you find
   - **Tools**: Hugging Face (free), Google Colab (free GPU!)

2. **Fake News Detector**
   - Use existing datasets (Kaggle)

   - Analyze limitations/errors
   - **Ethics focus**: When does it fail? Who's affected?

3. **AI Carbon Footprint Calculator**
   - Research model training costs
   - Build simple calculator
   - Visualize environmental impact
   - **Tools**: Python, free datasets

**Intermediate:**
4. **Fairness Audit of Public AI**
   - Test ChatGPT/Gemini for bias
   - Systematic prompt testing
   - Document findings
   - **Ethics**: Contribute to public knowledge!

5. **Accessibility AI Tool**
   - Image captioning for visually impaired
   - Use pre-trained models
   - Focus on underserved use case
   - **Tools**: Hugging Face Transformers

- **Kaggle Notebooks**: Free compute + datasets

## ‚ö†Ô∏è **Responsible Research Practices:**

### **DO:**
‚úÖ **Always cite sources** (even in personal projects)
‚úÖ **Consider bias** in every dataset/model
‚úÖ **Think about impact** before building
‚úÖ **Use public datasets** (Kaggle, Hugging Face, Google Dataset Search)
‚úÖ **Share learnings** (blog posts help others!)
‚úÖ **Ask for feedback** (communities love helping students)
‚úÖ **Respect privacy** (never scrape personal data)
‚úÖ **Acknowledge limitations** (be honest about what doesn't work)

### **DON'T:**
‚ùå **Scrape data without permission**
‚ùå **Deploy AI without safety checks**
‚ùå **Ignore environmental costs** (track compute usage)
‚ùå **Claim expertise you don't have** (be a learner!)
‚ùå **Build surveillance tools** (face recognition for tracking, etc.)
‚ùå **Ignore bias** ("the model said it" isn't an excuse)
‚ùå **Use AI to cheat** (obviously!)
