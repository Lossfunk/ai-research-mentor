## Venue-Specific Checklists

### NeurIPS 2024/2025
- [ ] Ethics impact statement (mandatory, separate section)
- [ ] Checklist covering: datasets, code, compute, broader impacts, theory/proofs
- [ ] Limitations and societal impact discussion
- [ ] Reproducibility: hyperparameters, seeds, statistical significance tests
- [ ] Funding disclosure

### ACL/EMNLP 2024/2025
- [ ] Mandatory "Limitations" section (unnumbered, before references)
- [ ] Ethics statement if using human data or dual-use potential
- [ ] Responsible NLP checklist (covers A1-A14, B1-B7)
- [ ] Computational budget in appendix
- [ ] License for released artifacts

### ICLR 2025
- [ ] Reproducibility statement (code, data, hyperparameters)
- [ ] Ethics review trigger check (human subjects, dual-use, environmental impact)
- [ ] Broader impact discussion (can be in appendix)
- [ ] Conflict of interest disclosure
